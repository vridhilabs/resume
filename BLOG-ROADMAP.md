# growth marketing blog Roadmap

Marketing wiki-style content for founders, marketers, and agency owners.

---

## Writing Style (Based on Modern GTM Publications)

- **Conversational but expert** — Write like you're explaining to a smart friend
- **Structure with purpose** — Clear headings, short paragraphs, bullet points
- **Examples over theory** — Show, don't just tell
- **Actionable insights** — Every post should have a "try this" moment
- **No jargon dumps** — Define terms when first used
- **Visual-friendly** — Tables, lists, and clear sections

## Content Guidelines

### Structure Template
```markdown
---
title: '...'
publishedAt: 'YYYY-MM-DD'
summary: 'One-sentence description of value delivered.'
---

## Opening Hook (2-3 sentences)
Why this matters *right now*.

## The Problem (1 paragraph)
What most people get wrong.

## The Framework/Strategy (3-5 sections)
Actionable content with examples.

## Real-World Example (1 section)
Case study, anonymized client story, or public example.

## Action Items (bulleted)
3-5 things to do this week.

## Summary (2-3 sentences)
Reinforce the core insight.
```

### Writing Rules
- **No "In today's fast-paced digital landscape"** — ZERO fluff openings
- **Lead with insight, not credentials** — No "We at Vridhi Labs have found..."
- **Every section under 200 words** — Keep it scannable
- **End with action** — Every post should end with "Try this:"
- **No AI-slop phrases** — "Delve into", "In the realm of", "It's worth noting"
